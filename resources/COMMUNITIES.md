## How to actually benefit from communities

1. **Lurk for 2 weeks.** Learn the vibe.
2. **Ask ONE thoughtful question per week.** Include: what you tried, what broke, your hypothesis.
3. **After month 2, answer ONE question per week.** Beginner-level. You know more than you think.
4. **Share your projects.** Not begging for likes — just "I built X, here's what I learned."
5. **Post your blog posts / capstone.** People on Discord will send them to friends.

The goal isn't clout. The goal is to become **findable**. When a recruiter sees your name in three different AI communities + a public capstone + a blog, you're no longer a resume — you're a person.
